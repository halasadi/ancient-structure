Exploratory analysis of Haak Human Origins data

From the Haak + Human Origins dataset, we extracted overall 403 samples (both modern and ancient) that were used in Figure 1 (Haak et al). There were in total 21 ancient populations with varying sample sizes and 23 modern populations from which the samples were considered.
